# Privacy Policy for PW Control

**Effective Date:** July 2, 2026

At **PW Control**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles user data.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or usage data.** 

All features in the extension operate entirely within your local browser environment.

## 2. Local Storage Usage

The extension uses the standard browser local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) for the sole purpose of saving your preferences:
- Preferred video playback speed.
- Custom keyboard shortcut configurations.
- Speed snap presets.
- Hiding toggle choices (e.g., whether Live Chat or Notes are hidden).

This data is stored **locally on your device** and is never uploaded to any servers or shared with third parties.

## 3. Remote Code

The extension **does not use remote code**. All executable files, scripts, and stylesheets are bundled locally within the extension package itself, ensuring high security and compliance with store policies.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published directly in our GitHub repository and updated in the extension package.
